CRITICAL CHANGE

⚖️ New Wages Definition — 50% Rule (CoW S.2(y))

Basic + DA must be ≥ 50% of CTC. If not, PF base is adjusted upward. Employers who have suppressed Basic to reduce PF are now non-compliant — flagged automatically.

CRITICAL CHANGE

⚙️ PF Recalculation on New Wages Base (CoSS)

PF contributions computed on adjusted wages per the new definition, not just Basic+DA. This tool auto-recalculates PF when the 50% threshold is breached and flags the delta.

NEW PROVISION

📄 Gratuity at 1 Year — Fixed-Term Employees (CoSS S.53)

Fixed-term contract employees are eligible for gratuity after 1 year (vs. 5 years for regular). Maximum gratuity raised to ₹20 lakhs.

NEW PROVISION

🕐 OT Limits & 2× Rate — OSH Code S.25

Maximum 12-hour shift cap (including OT). Weekly limit 48 hours. OT wages at 2× ordinary rate. Breaches flagged as critical compliance issues.

PENDING GAZETTE

📱 Gig & Platform Workers — CoSS S.113-114

Social security coverage for gig/platform workers mandated; state schemes pending notification. This tool flags gig workers and tracks for future compliance.

PENDING GAZETTE

📅 Leave Carry-Forward Cap — OSH Code S.32

Earned leave accrual: 1 day per 20 days worked. Carry-forward capped at 30 days (draft provision). Leave encashment on separation.
